Lentils pot

A hot recipie for the winter nigths

Ingridients

  1. Lentils
  2. Carots
  3. Olive Oil
  4. Garlic
  5. Chorizo
  6. Olive Oil

Preparation

The lentils must be soked acording the follow table:

Soaking times
Type of Lentils Soaking Time
Brown and Green 8 hours
Red 4 hours
Frenc Not required
Black Beluga 2 hours

Cut the garlic, paprika and onion in the mixer. Fry them with oil during 10 minutes at 100ºC and 4 Speed. Add the lentils, some water and chorizo. Cook at 110ºC during 30 minutes.

Serving

Serve in a boal. You can froze few portions for a future emergency.

The Lentils is a dish from central Spain although is eaten everywhere.
